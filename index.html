Welcome to IRA Estates

Nestled along the pristine shores of Benaulim, South Goa, IRA Estates offers an exclusive collection of eight 4BHK villas — each a private world unto itself.

IRA Estates was founded on a singular belief: that a home should be an experience, not merely an address. Every villa at IRA Estates is designed to embody the spirit of Goa — open, luminous, and deeply connected to the sea and sky.

With over a decade of crafting premium residences across Goa, IRA Estates has cultivated a reputation for uncompromising quality, thoughtful design, and an abiding respect for the natural landscape. Each project is a testament to our passion for creating spaces that endure.

IRA Estates comprises just eight villas — an intentional decision to preserve the intimacy, exclusivity, and tranquility that every discerning resident deserves. Here, you are not a number. You are a neighbour.

Welcome to a life perfectly balanced between luxury and nature.

Benaulim is one of South Goa's best-kept secrets — a pristine stretch of golden sand where the Arabian Sea meets a quieter, more considered way of living. Far from the crowds, yet close to everything.

Coconut groves sway in the sea breeze, fishing boats dot the horizon at sunrise, and evenings carry the sound of waves through open windows. This is Goa as it was meant to be experienced.

Just minutes from Benaulim Beach, IRA Estates places you at the intersection of Goa's finest pleasures — sun-warmed sands, world-class dining, water sports, and a thriving arts and culture scene, all within effortless reach.

Dudhsagar Falls is 60 km away. Margao railway station lies 10 km to the north. Goa International Airport connects you to the world in just 40 minutes. Every convenience, perfectly positioned around your private paradise.

South Goa's tranquility stays with you long after the tide has turned. IRA Estates simply makes it your permanent address.

Private Pool & Landscaped Gardens

Each villa is gifted its own private swimming pool and individually landscaped garden — a personal sanctuary designed for relaxation, entertaining, and the quiet pleasures of Goa living.

Premium Interiors & Fittings

Kohler and Jaguar sanitaryware, Legrand modular switches, UPVC Fenesta windows, and Jindal Steel structural elements are standard across all villas.

Italian marble and premium stone flooring, modular kitchens with hob and chimney, and ambient mood lighting complete the picture of understated luxury.

4 BHK · Spacious & Airy

Each villa offers four generously proportioned bedrooms, multiple living areas, and outdoor terraces designed to blur the line between inside and the Goa outdoors.

High ceilings, cross-ventilation, and thoughtful orientation ensure every room stays cool, bright, and connected to the natural world around it.
